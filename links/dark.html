Dark Type Pokémon

The Dark type (Japanese: むしタイプ Dark type) is one of the eighteen types. Notable Trainers who specialize in Dark-type Pokémon are Darksy of Azalea Town, Aaron of the Sinnoh Elite Four, Burgh of Castelia City, and Viola of Santalune City. Prior to changes in Generation IV, all Dark-type moves were physical, but they may now also be special depending on the attack.

Absol

Absol is a quadruped, mammalian Pokémon with a dark blue to gray body covered in white fur. It has a ruff around its neck and chest and a tuft.

Poochyena

Poochyena is a quadruped Pokémon similar in appearance to hyenas and canines. Its body is primarily gray, with a black face, throat, feet, and belly.

Umbreon

Umbreon has a sleek black body with four slender legs and crimson eyes. There are two pairs of pointed teeth visible when its mouth is open.

Dark Pokémon are usually amongst the first Pokémon types a Trainer can find in the wild, with the exception of Pokémon Black and White. While they generally evolve at very early levels compared to most other Pokémon, a few Dark-type evolutionary lines such as the Volcarona evolutionary line are fully evolved at a much later level. The type varies from strong to weak. Depending on the Dark Pokémon, they can prove to be a nuisance with stat altering moves or hard hitting attacks such as Megahorn, X-Scissor, Signal Beam and Dark Buzz. There is only one Legendary Dark-type Pokémon, Genesect.

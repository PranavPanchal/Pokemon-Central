The Fierce Monsters

Dragon Type Pokémon

The Dragon type (Japanese: むしタイプ Dragon type) is one of the eighteen types. Notable Trainers who specialize in Dragon-type Pokémon are Dragonsy of Azalea Town, Aaron of the Sinnoh Elite Four, Burgh of Castelia City, and Viola of Santalune City. Prior to changes in Generation IV, all Dragon-type moves were physical, but they may now also be special depending on the attack.

Dragonair

Dragonair is a long, serpentine Pokémon covered in blue scales along its back and a bright white underside. Its violet eyes are dark and glossy.

Druddigon

Druddigon is a large, blue, draconic Pokémon with medium-sized wings shaped like thistle leaves. Its bright red head is harder than rock and is very rough.

Shelgon

Shelgon is a quadruped Pokémon that resembles a pupal stage. There are bone structures covering its body, which form a shell with two openings and three ridges on top.

Dragon Pokémon are usually amongst the first Pokémon types a Trainer can find in the wild, with the exception of Pokémon Black and White. While they generally evolve at very early levels compared to most other Pokémon, a few Dragon-type evolutionary lines such as the Volcarona evolutionary line are fully evolved at a much later level. The type varies from strong to weak. Depending on the Dragon Pokémon, they can prove to be a nuisance with stat altering moves or hard hitting attacks such as Megahorn, X-Scissor, Signal Beam and Dragon Buzz. There is only one Legendary Dragon-type Pokémon, Genesect.

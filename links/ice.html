Ice Type Pokémon

The Ice type (Japanese: むしタイプ Ice type) is one of the eighteen types. Notable Trainers who specialize in Ice-type Pokémon are Icesy of Azalea Town, Aaron of the Sinnoh Elite Four, Burgh of Castelia City, and Viola of Santalune City. Prior to changes in Generation IV, all Ice-type moves were physical, but they may now also be special depending on the attack.

Froslass

Froslass is a humanoid Pokémon with a hollow torso that resembles a kimono. It wears a red band around its waist, reminiscent of an Obi. Its body lacks feet and it floats in the air.

Glalie

Glalie is a spherical Pokémon that consists of a black rock core covered by ice. The icy covering has several holes and spaces across this Pokémon’s body. Blue eyes and a mouth with flat teeth.

Mamoswine

Mamoswine resembles a wooly mammoth crossed with a boar. Its brown fur is very thick to endure harsh cold and protect from snow and ice.

Ice Pokémon are usually amongst the first Pokémon types a Trainer can find in the wild, with the exception of Pokémon Black and White. While they generally evolve at very early levels compared to most other Pokémon, a few Ice-type evolutionary lines such as the Volcarona evolutionary line are fully evolved at a much later level. The type varies from strong to weak. Depending on the Ice Pokémon, they can prove to be a nuisance with stat altering moves or hard hitting attacks such as Megahorn, X-Scissor, Signal Beam and Ice Buzz. There is only one Legendary Ice-type Pokémon, Genesect.

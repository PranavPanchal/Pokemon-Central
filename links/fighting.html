Fighting Type Pokémon

The Fighting type (Japanese: むしタイプ Fighting type) is one of the eighteen types. Notable Trainers who specialize in Fighting-type Pokémon are Fightingsy of Azalea Town, Aaron of the Sinnoh Elite Four, Burgh of Castelia City, and Viola of Santalune City. Prior to changes in Generation IV, all Fighting-type moves were physical, but they may now also be special depending on the attack.

Hitmontop

Hitmontop is a vaguely humanoid Pokémon capable of spinning on its head. Its main body is spherical and mostly blue with a brown triangular patch on its chest.

Machop

Machop is a humanoid, bipedal Pokémon that has gray skin. There are three brown ridges on top of its head, and it has large red eyes and relatively flat face.

Primeape

Primeape is a bipedal Pokémon with a round body covered in whitish, shaggy fur. There is usually has a stress mark on the left side of its forehead, and it has triangular ears.

Fighting Pokémon are usually amongst the first Pokémon types a Trainer can find in the wild, with the exception of Pokémon Black and White. While they generally evolve at very early levels compared to most other Pokémon, a few Fighting-type evolutionary lines such as the Volcarona evolutionary line are fully evolved at a much later level. The type varies from strong to weak. Depending on the Fighting Pokémon, they can prove to be a nuisance with stat altering moves or hard hitting attacks such as Megahorn, X-Scissor, Signal Beam and Fighting Buzz. There is only one Legendary Fighting-type Pokémon, Genesect.
